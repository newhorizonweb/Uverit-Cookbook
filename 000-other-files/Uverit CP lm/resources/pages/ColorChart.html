Why is it so important?

Having too many colors in the design is overwhelming to the senses. They can cause distraction from important elements.

That's why you want to keep everything balanced, as all things should be.

Should you keep your color aspect ratio exactly as shown here? No, I encourage you or your designer to try what it will look best on the final product, but please remeber this chart.

This way you can keep it simple and tasteful.
